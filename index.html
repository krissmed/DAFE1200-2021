Project Rebound

Reuse, Recycle, Rebound

What is the problem?

Seriously, it’s a huge problem, both for humans and nature (kilde). E-waste production worldwide is on par with Sweden's waste production overall (klide). But, contrary to most other waste, e-waste contains dangerous materials which are very difficult to handle when thrown away and really difficult to recycle (kilde). The sad part is most of the components are still usable and can either be used in another equivalent product or used as part to fix another device.

What is the solution?

Our solution to the problem is to make it easier for you and me to get new and used devices, whichever you prefer. We can provide you a device, whether you like to be on the bleeding edge, or don’t mind lagging a few years behind. We can provide devices, such as phones, watches, headphones, laptops, tablets etc. You can rent them and use them as your own for as long as you want and when you want to switch, you send your used device back to us and we will send you your new device.
